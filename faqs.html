FAQs

Welcome to our FAQ for the ultimate outdoor experience! Our adult summer camp teaches people how to camp and be confident in the great outdoors. Our expert instructors are here to answer your questions about everything from pitching a tent to starting a campfire. Let us guide you through everything you need to know for an unforgettable camping experience.

No, there isn't any wifi or cell service for miles. At Camp Rewind, we feel like disconnecting from one’s modern life is essential to getting the most out of your camp experience. We feel technology is a huge contributor to this disconnect. There are a few landlines available in certain buildings for emergencies. That being said, if you somehow find a way to use your devices, we aren’t going to confiscate it. After all this is an ADULT summer camp.

If you opt into the premium package you don't need to worry about much. But even if you didn't, a fairly comprehensive list will be provided in your conformation email of items that we strongly recommend you bring. There are both working bathrooms and showers on site. Also 3 meals per day is included in the admission price.

Traditional camp food that we prepare or you bring your own. If you have any serious food allergies we suggest you bring your own food, because Camp Rewind cannot guarantee that the food we serve is completely absent of potentially harmful ingredients. Three meals will be served each day, feel free to help yourself. These meals are covered in everyone’s admission cost.

This is a no kids weekend! 18 years of age or older is the requirement, 21 years of age or older is preferred. But other than that every summer we’ve hosted Camp Rewind, we always encounter and befriended all sorts of people from every walk of life.

The safety and well-being of our campers are our top priorities. We maintain a comprehensive safety protocol that includes trained staff, first aid facilities, and emergency response procedures. Our activities and facilities undergo regular inspections to ensure they meet the highest safety standards. Additionally, we provide all participants with detailed safety guidelines and conduct thorough orientation sessions to promote a safe and enjoyable environment.
