About

Sometimes you gotta disconnect to reconnect.

Drew and Jenn are two adventurous friends who love spending time in the great outdoors. They often talk about how much they miss the summer camps they attended as kids and wonder why there aren't more opportunities for adults to experience the same sense of community and adventure.

That's why they decided to create their own summer camp for adults, where participants can leave their responsibilities behind and connect with like-minded people while learning new outdoor skills like fishing, foraging for mushrooms, archery, and building shelters. They are excited to share their passion for the outdoors with others and create an unforgettable experience for all who attend.

The Campground

Camp Rewind sites boast stunning views of the surrounding landscape and is conveniently located near all the activities. With easy access to clean water and modern amenities like fire pits and restrooms, it offers the ideal balance of comfort and natural beauty.

Scenic Oregon

Oregon is a stunning and diverse wilderness that offers the perfect backdrop for adults to learn about camping and outdoor activities. From the rugged coastline to the Cascade Mountains, the state is home to some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in the country. Whether you're interested in hiking, kayaking, fishing, or foraging, the wilderness of Oregon provides endless opportunities for adventure and learning. The mild climate and welcoming local communities make it an ideal destination for those looking to escape the bustle of city life and connect with nature.
