Activities

At our outdoor camp, we offer a wide variety of activities that are perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. From guided hikes to horseback riding, fishing to blacksmithing, there is something for everyone to enjoy. If you have an interest in the creative arts, you can try your hand at whittling, basket weaving, knitting, or leatherworking. For those who prefer more physical activities, we offer bouldering and climbing, ziplining, and archery. Our expert instructors are also available to teach survival skills such as shelter building, foraging, animal tracking, and plant identification. If you're feeling more relaxed, you can join us for yoga sessions, with the option to have some fun with goats, or enjoy a singalong around the campfire. Outdoor cooking is also a great way to experience the camp's surroundings and practice your culinary skills. Whether you're a seasoned outdoors enthusiast or new to camping, our camp has something for everyone to learn, explore, and have fun.

About

Our outdoor camp was created by a group of adults who longed for an opportunity to learn and experience the joys of the great outdoors. Growing up, they never had the chance to learn basic outdoor skills like knot-tying, fishing, or tent pitching, and they wanted to fill that gap in their lives. As they looked around at the abundance of camps available for kids, they realized that there weren't many options for adults to learn these skills in a supportive and immersive environment. With a shared passion for nature and a desire to connect with like-minded people, they decided to create an adult outdoor camp that would offer a wide range of outdoor activities. The founders drew upon their own experiences and passions to curate an exciting lineup of workshops and adventures, designed to provide a fun and engaging learning experience for participants of all skill levels. They believe that it's never too late to learn something new and that everyone should have the opportunity to discover the beauty and wonder of the natural world. Today, our outdoor camp has become a popular destination for adults seeking to broaden their horizons and connect with nature. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a complete novice, our camp offers a welcoming and supportive environment where you can learn, explore, and have fun in the great outdoors.
