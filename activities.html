Activities

They say a picture is worth a thousand words, but don’t take their word for it. See for yourself...

‘This all sounds great,’ you might find yourself saying. ‘But what is there to do there?’

To answer your question in short: ‘A LOT!!.’ And growing...we’re not even close to the limit of our expansion. Every single year we’ve put on Camp Rewind at least 2 more activities have been added for the following summer.

Kayaking
Swimming
Fishing

Camp Rewind, conveniently located close to multiple bodies of water, our camp offers the perfect setting for aquatic adventures. Dive into a world of relaxation and exploration as you immerse yourself in crystal-clear lakes, meandering rivers, and tranquil ponds. Whether you're seeking the thrill of swimming, the tranquility of fishing, or the exhilaration of kayaking; Camp Rewind provides endless opportunities to embrace the water.

Experience the joy of casting your line and reeling in your catch, or experience, having never fished before, the joy of reeling your first catch ever. With nature as your playground and learing how to navigate through it and embrace it; life just always seems to get better.

Life’s just funny that way, innit?

Camp Rewind unlocks the inner adventurer in everyone. Our campers gain outdoor skills and knowledge, becoming true enthusiasts. With expert-led workshops, hands-on experiences, and personalized guidance, campers grow in a learning environment that fosters confidence and creativity.

Camp Rewind equips its campers with new skills, appreciation, and connection to the outdoors. Through survival training, rock climbing, navigation, and outdoor cooking lessons, campers gain self-reliance and resilience that stays with them even after leaving camp.

Camp Rewind is about people as much as activities. Forge lifelong connections around the campfire and under starlit skies. Our diverse tapestry of campers celebrates each individual, making our camp a special place.

Join our Camp Rewind family, come as you are and leave with new experiences. Whether you're a city dweller or free spirit, seeking new adventures or a joyful escape, Camp Rewind exceeds your wildest expectations.

Spaces are limited, so secure your spot today and embark on a summer journey like no other. Unleash your inner child, embrace the extraordinary, and let Camp Rewind be the backdrop to the best summer of your life. We can't wait to welcome you home.
