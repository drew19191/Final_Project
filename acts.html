Fishing

Camp Rewind, conveniently located close to multiple bodies of water, our camp offers the perfect setting for aquatic adventures. Dive into a world of relaxation and exploration as you immerse yourself in crystal-clear lakes, meandering rivers, and tranquil ponds. Whether you're seeking the thrill of swimming, the tranquility of fishing, or the exhilaration of kayaking; Camp Rewind provides endless opportunities to embrace the water. Experience the joy of casting your line and reeling in your catch, or experience, having never fished before, the joy of reeling your first catch ever. With nature as your playground and learing how to navigate through it and embrace it; life just always seems to get better.

At Camp Rewind, we believe that everyone has an inner adventurer waiting to be unleashed. Our campers embark on a transformative journey where they not only experience thrilling outdoor activities but also gain the skills and knowledge to become true outdoor enthusiasts. Through a combination of hands-on experiences, expert-led workshops, and personalized guidance from our passionate staff, campers immerse themselves in a learning environment that fosters growth, confidence and creativity. Whether it's mastering the art of wilderness survival, honing their rock climbing techniques, navigating through challenging terrains, or discovering the secrets of outdoor cooking, our campers leave Camp Rewind equipped with a newfound understanding and appreciation for the great outdoors. With each activity and lesson, they develop a sense of self-reliance, resilience, and a deep connection with nature that will stay with them long after their time at Camp Rewind has come to an end.

Camp Rewind is not just about all our lovely and varied activities; it's about the people who make it truly special. Forge lifelong connections as you gather around the campfire, swap stories under starlit skies, and take a brief, calculated step back from the modern world. Our camp is a tapestry of diverse backgrounds and perspectives, and we celebrate each and every individual that puts up a tent.
Come as you are, and leave as a part of our Camp Rewind family. Whether you're a city dweller yearning for a taste of the wild, a free spirit seeking new adventures, or simply someone in need of a joyful escape, we guarantee that Camp Rewind will exceed your wildest expectations. Spaces are limited, so secure your spot today and embark on a summer journey like no other. Unleash your inner child, embrace the extraordinary, and let Camp Rewind be the backdrop to the best summer of your life. We can't wait to welcome you home.
